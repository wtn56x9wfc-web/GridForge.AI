FAQ

Direct answers to common questions—no filler.

What makes GridForge different?

Focused UX, strong defaults, and succinct writing standards. We use proven patterns from top AI orgs: short paragraphs, clear structure, and outcome-driven prompts.

Do you store my data?

Inputs are used to generate drafts and improve the experience. Sensitive data handling follows common enterprise guidelines. Configure internal policies as needed.

Can I customize tone and templates?

Yes. Define tone, message type, and goals. GridForge aligns to your voice while keeping messages professional and concise.

Is this only for email?

No. It supports emails, LinkedIn DMs, follow-ups, and inbound replies—always emphasizing clarity and strong calls-to-action.

If you know what good looks like—concise memos, clear asks, measurable outcomes—you’ll feel at home here.
