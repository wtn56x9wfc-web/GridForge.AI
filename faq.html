FAQ

Straightforward answers—no filler.

What makes GridForge different?

A focused surface, strong defaults, and concise writing. It’s the same product discipline you see on modern AI sites: short paragraphs, clear structure, and outcomes first.

Do you store my data?

Inputs are used to generate drafts and improve your experience. Handle sensitive data according to your internal policies— GridForge is designed to work within enterprise guardrails.

Can I tune tone and templates?

Yes. Define tone, message type, and goals. Drafts stay professional, brief, and context-aware.

Is it only for email?

No—email, LinkedIn DMs, follow-ups, and inbound replies are supported, all with the same emphasis on clarity and a clear ask.
