FAQ

Straight answers. No filler. No coping.

What is GridForge?

GridForge is a workflow for generating professional outreach: you provide structured inputs, it drafts clear messaging, and you review + send. It’s built for signal over noise.

What makes GridForge different?

Strong defaults, clean structure, and concise writing. It’s designed to produce messages that feel human and get to the point.

Is it only for email?

No. Email, LinkedIn DMs, follow-ups, and inbound replies are supported. The structure adapts, but the rules stay the same: relevance + clarity + a clear ask.

How do I get better outputs?

Better inputs. Add one specific relevance hook, keep constraints clear, and don’t ask for five things at once.

Do you store my data?

Treat GridForge like any outbound tool: don’t paste secrets or regulated data. If you operate under strict compliance, keep inputs aligned with your internal policies.

Can I customize tone and templates?

Yes. Define tone, message type, and objectives. For consistency, reuse constraints that work.

What’s “message type”?

It tells GridForge how the message should behave: Email is formal, LinkedIn is short, follow-ups assume context, inbound replies are direct.

What should I put in “Additional context”?

Facts that increase relevance: timing, constraints, references. One strong detail beats a paragraph of fluff.

How does Bulk Generator work?

Upload a CSV where each row is a recipient. GridForge generates one message per row.

What headers does Bulk require?

name, company, title, email, notes, industry, goals, messageType

Does GridForge guarantee replies?

No. Nothing does. GridForge improves clarity and relevance. Offer, targeting, and timing still matter.

Is this “AI writing” detectable?

Only if it’s generic. Keep it short, specific, and human. Long intros and corporate fluff are the giveaway.
