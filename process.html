How it works

GridForge turns messy outreach into a clean, repeatable workflow. You provide the right inputs, it generates a tight draft, and you review + send with confidence.

Less fluff. More replies.

```

1) Provide inputs

Business, recipient, objective, and context. Clarity beats volume every time.

2) Generate a draft

Professional copy tuned to intent, industry, and the recipient’s role.

3) Review & ship

Edit fast, keep what works, and send. No overthinking.

What you should have ready before generating:

Recipient clarity

Name, company, role, and what they care about (pain, priority, KPI).

Your offer

What you do in one sentence. If you can’t explain it simply, fix that first.

A single objective

Intro, call, meeting, referral, or reply. One ask per message. Always.

Single Draft workflow

Use this when you want one high-quality message you can send immediately. GridForge keeps the structure tight: context → relevance → ask → easy close.

Set the scene

Business and recipient basics anchor the message in real context instead of generic AI filler.

Define intent

Email, LinkedIn DM, follow-up, or inbound reply. Each has different pacing and tone rules.

Add constraints

Constraints make drafts better. “Under 90 words” beats “make it sound good.”

Bulk Generator workflow

Bulk is for structured outreach at scale. One row equals one recipient. No shortcuts.

Download the template

Headers must match or outputs degrade. Don’t freestyle this.

Fill rows cleanly

Short, factual notes win. “Hiring SDRs” beats a paragraph of fluff.

Generate & export

Preview rows, generate drafts, export results, and send with your own system.
```
